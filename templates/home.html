How to use this website?

General introduction

On the visualisations tab, a dataset can be uploaded, which will be visualised.
Then, the desired visualisation can be selected from the dropdown menu, after which the visualisation will appear.

Uploading the data

The uploaded data must be in .csv format.
The following columns must be present in the data:
"date, fromId, fromEmail, fromJobtitle, toId, toEmail, toJobtitle, messageType, sentiment"

Choosing the visualisation(s)

Use the drop down menu (or number key) to select the visualisation(s) you want to see.
You can choose to show a Radial Nodes and Links graph or a Force Directed graph.
