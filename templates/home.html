How to use this website?

General introduction

On the visualisations tab, a dataset can be uploaded, which will be visualised.
Then, the desired visualisations will appear.

Interactivity

Underneath the visualisations you can find a date slider and a job category sorter.
The visualisations only show nodes and edges that are included in the range of the slider.
The category sorter allows you to only show edges and nodes of a specified occupation.

Uploading the data

The uploaded data must be in .csv format.
The following columns must be present in the data:
"date, fromId, fromEmail, fromJobtitle, toId, toEmail, toJobtitle, messageType, sentiment"
